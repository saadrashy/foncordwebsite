Founder and CEO Speaks

Foncord Group

I’m excited to announce the QatZam Mining Project, a groundbreaking partnership between Zambia and Qatar focused on copper and gold mining. This initiative will integrate Qatar’s advanced mining technologies with Zambia’s rich mineral resources to drive sustainable practices and operational efficiency. Our goal is to minimize environmental impact, boost local economies, and set new standards in the industry. By working together, we aim to create a model for responsible and innovative mining that benefits both our nations and the global community.
